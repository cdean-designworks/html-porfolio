Welcome to Heatherwood

Heatherwood is a vibrant, family-friendly community located in the scenic northern corridor of McKinney, Texas. As part of the Prosper Independent School District, our neighborhood offers a peaceful suburban lifestyle with easy access to major highways, shopping, dining, and entertainment.

The Heatherwood HOA is committed to preserving the beauty, safety, and integrity of our neighborhood while fostering a strong sense of community among residents. We encourage active participation and open communication to ensure Heatherwood remains a place we’re all proud to call home.

Community Highlights

  • Beautifully maintained homes with spacious layouts and inviting curb appeal
  • Tree-lined streets, greenbelts, and walking trails for outdoor enjoyment
  • A private community pool and playground for residents and guests
  • Seasonal events and neighborhood gatherings that bring our community together

HOA Services & Resources

  • Architectural Review & Guidelines: Ensuring consistent standards and property value
  • Amenity Access: Pool key requests, playground maintenance, and common area updates
  • Resident Portal: Pay dues, submit requests, and stay informed
  • Community News: Updates on events, meetings, and neighborhood initiatives
