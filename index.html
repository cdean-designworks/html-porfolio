About Me

As a passionate web designer with a background as a Networking Solution Architect, I bring a unique blend of technical expertise and creative vision to craft visually stunning, user-friendly digital experiences. My certifications in CompTIA A+, Network+, and Aruba Certified Design Associate (ACDA) have honed my skills in problem-solving and systems integration, which I now apply to building responsive, intuitive websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Through my Udemy course "The Complete Full-Stack Web Development Bootcamp" and hands-on projects like this portfolio, I transform ideas into dynamic, engaging interfaces with meticulous attention to detail, leveraging tools like Visual Studio Code and AI-driven solutions to bring innovative designs to life.
